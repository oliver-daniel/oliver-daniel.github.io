About Me

I'm a developer with four years of experience, both inside and outside the classroom. I'm dedicated to using my skills to make my community, city, and world a better place through innovation and communication.

I was on the charter executive board for the Toronto Youth Network, a non-profit devoted to helping ambitious Toronto youth get a head-start on their professional lives, and currently serve as Co-Chair. That experience was probably among the most influential in my life so far; since I started with TYN, I've started pursuing my passions, including software development, with unprecedented vigour.

Please feel free to reach out. I look forward to meeting you!

Of course, technical skills, such as those I've listed below, aren't everything. I make sure to find the passion in every project and position I take on, and to always be communicative and warm to my peers, in order to do the best job that I can. My goal is always to be a great employee and teammate, and that means being responsible, honest, and someone you can rely on to get things done on schedule.
